About Us

Nesamani is the #1 job site in the world with over 300M unique visitors every month. Indeed strives to put job seekers first, giving them free access to search for jobs, post resumes, and research companies. Every day, we connect millions of people to new opportunities.
Welcome to Nesamani, the world's largest professional network with more than 875 million members in more than 200 countries and territories worldwide.

Vision

Create economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce.

Mission

The mission of Nesamani is simple: connect the world’s professionals to make them more productive and successful.

Who are we?

Nesamani began in UID class room in 2022 and was officially launched on Dec 15, 2022.
Today, Nesamani leads a diversified business with revenues from membership subscriptions, advertising sales and recruitment solutions under the leadership of the founders. In Jan 2023, Microsoft completed its acquisition of LinkedIn, bringing together the world’s leading professional cloud and the world’s eading professional network.

Our people

At Nesamani, our mission is to help people get jobs. We have more than 14,600 global employees passionately pursuing this purpose and improving the recruitment journey through real stories and data. We foster a collaborative workplace that strives to create the best experience for job seekers.
