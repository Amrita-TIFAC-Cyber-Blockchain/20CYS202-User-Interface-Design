Amrita Pharma Supply chain management (APSCM) refers to the process of overseeing and coordinating the various tasks and activities involved in the production, handling, and distribution of goods and services.


Our website is a digital platform that provides information and resources related to SCM, including supply chain planning, logistics, procurement, warehousing, and transportation.


It may also offer tools and services for optimizing supply chain operations, such as inventory management, demand forecasting, and supplier relationship management. The goal of a supply chain management website is to help businesses streamline and improve their supply chain processes, resulting in increased efficiency, cost savings, and customer satisfaction.
