    Welcome to Amrita's very own E-Wallet !
    With the constant agenda of getting Bigger and Better, this time we've brought another innovation as a leap to step up and go cashless! This Anokha, we are launching our latest initiative in support of Digital India- EWallet. Register at the e-wallet desks with your *ID CARDS* and recharge them like usual with any amount of your choice. They can be used at any of our food stalls, and can be recharged any number of times. At the end of Anokha, you are free to collect the balance amount in your e-wallet account from the desk!
