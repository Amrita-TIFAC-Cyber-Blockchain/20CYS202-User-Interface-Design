About Us

Groceries Organic Store

Our aim is to reduce the delivery time taken.And encourage the local shops in anytimes like lockdown where local merchants lost their income.so,we will provide a platform to them to display their products without any charges.At the same time we will ensure that the product quality and also promise you that products are sanitized before you recieve them.

No need to wait

Our local-to-table concept emphasizes on getting the fresh produce directly from local shops to your tables within less time, hence you can enjoy shopping in times like lockdown.

Know Your local varities

We want you to know exactly what is popular shop and varities in your locality with good quality. You’re welcome you to provide feed-back.

Improving local merchants’ Livelihood

Slowly but sure, by cutting the complex supply chain and food system, we hope to improve the welfare of small local merchants who are effected by times like lockdown.
