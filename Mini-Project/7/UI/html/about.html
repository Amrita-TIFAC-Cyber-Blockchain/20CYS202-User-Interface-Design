About Kolect


Kolect is a stamp purchase website created by 3 students of the 2nd Semester of B.Tech Cyber Security.
We are a team of stamp collecting enthusiasts who have come together to provide a platform for fellow
collectors to buy and sell stamps from all over the world.


Our aim of the website is to make easy sales to the people who are willing to collect stamps and save them.
It also aims to reduce the amount people waiting in a queue for a stamp to send any post.
Our website offers a wide variety of stamps, from rare and valuable to more affordable options
for those just starting their collection. We are constantly updating our inventory to ensure that
we have something for every collector.


Rather they can purchase stamps from the website and use them for sending mails and posts which will
save their time. It is also an E-commerce portal for Philately lovers, collectors searching for Indian Stamps,
International Stamps, Postal History, Postal covers, Miniature sheets, Mint & Used singles, Errors, My stamps,
Thematic stamps and other related philatelic items. We have currently more than 45000 stamps online, also new
collections of stamps added weekly to our website. Once you become our registered customers we send periodic
newsletters on new arrivals and philatelic products matching your collecting interests.We also exclusively sell
imported stamps to the people who are interested in collecting other country's stamps.

In addition to buying and selling stamps, we also offer resources and information for collectors of all levels,
including articles on stamp collecting techniques, historical background on various stamps, and advice on building
and maintaining a collection.

We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service and are dedicated to ensuring that every transaction is a positive
experience for our customers. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.


Thank you for visiting our website and happy KOLECTing!
