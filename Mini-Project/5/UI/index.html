What is Student Hub ?

Student Hub is a social website for amrita students who are trying to showcase their talents to everyone and who try to find a partner for projects.

Media

This is a place where Photographies or Shortfilms can be posted by aspiring photographers.Students who logged in with their college email id can like and comment on their post. Weekly leaderboard will be updated to have a healthy competiton among students.

Partner

If you wanted to find a partner for your project or for a reasearch and also to find like minded people this feature is for you.

Build

Build yourself with working with others with the same interest.

Alumni

Interact with the alumnis and get details about their placement and stuffs.
